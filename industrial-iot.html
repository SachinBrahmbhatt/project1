Industrial Internet of Things

  1. Home
  2. Industrial Internet of Things

The Internet of Things (IoT) technology brings immense opportunities and with the right investments and processes, organizations can achieve transformative business results.

Most organizations that implement IoT-based solutions do not get it right. For a successful IoT implementation to start demonstrating results, KPIs and goals must be well-defined. Further, this should be measured by an expected outcome in line with the business process. Only then will an IoT implementation yield value and unlock the true potential of other such implementations.

Phoenixgen helps clients leverage purpose-built IoT solutions that deliver efficiencies across customer experience, business models, and operational processes at every stage of implementation. This effectively translates to delivering business values and optimizes operational costs and risks.

We offer a rapid IoT prototyping laboratory that enables quick visualization of IoT concepts. This provides a platform to integrate with partner technologies across our service offerings to enable IoT innovation in areas such as connected cars, smart homes, smart cities, smart workplaces, e-health, and usage-based insurance.

Some of our key focus areas include:

  • 100% safe and secure
  • Processing high-volume data and information streams from various devices/sensors
  • Leveraging machine learning to drive adaptive analytics and respond in real-time
  • Delivering cross-industry solutions and use cases to accelerate time-to-market
  • Providing API/Microservices layer to seamlessly integrate across touchpoints
  • IoT and data security
